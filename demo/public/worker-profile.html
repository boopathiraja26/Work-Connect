Help Center

Getting Started

Complete your profile with skills, experience, and UPI ID to start receiving orders. Make sure your availability is set to "Available" to appear in customer searches.

Payment & Earnings

Customers will pay you directly via UPI using your UPI ID. Once payment is received, you'll see a green success message with the payment screenshot (if uploaded by customer) in your orders list.

Ratings & Reviews

Maintain high ratings by completing orders on time and providing quality service. Good ratings help you get more orders from customers.
